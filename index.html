ScratchAddons

ScratchAddons is an open source project started by World_Languages aimed at creating a collection of browser extensions, userscripts and userstyles and combining them into one browser extension. It has two major motives: to help improve the look, feel, and function of the scratch website, and to collect and archive userstyles and userscripts because the Scratch Team is deleting them. Eventually, ScratchAddons will be a browser extension for Chrome and Firefox, but for now it is being developed on GitHub.
